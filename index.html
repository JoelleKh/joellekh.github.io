I was a founding team member at Oculi, a deep-tech semiconductor company developing a disruptive AI vision sensing architecture for real-time edge intelligence. Over 3.5 years I grew from Application Engineer to Lead Research Engineer, shipping production CV systems operating at up to 1,000 fps using Dynamic Vision Sensors.

What sets my profile apart is the depth across the full stack. I understand computer vision algorithms deeply. I train and deploy deep learning models with PyTorch and TensorFlow.
